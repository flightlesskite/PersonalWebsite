Interests

In terms of the field of research I am most interested in, I would say the advancements in VR and AR technologies are ones I find to be most fascinating. In particular, the applications used within entertainment (such as Video Games) are simply mind-blowing! To be able to create a level of immersion that previously only existed in fiction is such an incredible feat. In addition to this, I have developed an interest in robotics upon completing my Masters Final Project. With advancements in AI and the ability to replicate human-like qualities, I find myself excited to see what the future holds for the robotics field.

Outside of technology, I like to go for runs (on a sunny day), watch anime or learn some songs. My anime taste isn't the greatest but I like some emotional stories like Clannad and Little Busters! I am currently really into soul and gospel music but my first love was RnB back in the 2000s. I can play piano, guitar and sing. I used to also play the flute in secondary school but probably cannot remember how to do it anymore... I would say I am quite big into fashion and like to keep up with styles from all over the world. If I have a bit more free time, I do enjoy playing video games and playing tennis (again if the sun is out).
